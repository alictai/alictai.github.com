About Me

I am obsessed with design. How did it come to be that the software around us so strongly reflects our own human behavior, yet we still have such a difficult time creating positive interactions for our users? Let's make the fast-moving, run-by-technology, world around us easier to interact with - one click (or keystroke, touch, look, etc) at a time.

I love developers. I’m a program manager at Microsoft working on tools for customer engagement in Team Services and Team Foundation Server (VSTS and TFS) – software which enables developers to share code, track work, and ship software. I have a nifty Computer Science degree from Tufts University, and also have my fingers in several side projects.

I exist outside of work. I like to play board games, solve puzzles, praise Jesus, spin fire, knit or crochet while binge-watching Netflix, and hang upside-down.
